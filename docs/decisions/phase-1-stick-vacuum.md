## Notes

Bosch Unlimited 7 (BBS711-class, ~2,300 DKK) was eliminated as both over-budget AND the exact family that broke after 3 years for this household — same trigger / motor-bearing failure mode would likely repeat. Bosch Unlimited 9 and Serie 6 share the same PowerForAll architecture so the durability scores reflect that risk inheritance even on newer models. Samsung Jet 75E is the only sub-2,000 DKK candidate where both price AND a spec (200 W motor) were verifiable on the same retailer page; whiteaway also offers 365-day return + price-match which is a real safety net against early-failure risk. Electrolux 700 Animal is the only candidate with a verified runtime number (40 min). Xiaomi G20's headline 1,460 W is motor-input wattage, not airwatt suction — not directly comparable. Spare-parts availability assessed via Pricerunner cross-listings: Electrolux/AEG share parts pool (filters 100–180 DKK, batteries 169–399 DKK) is the strongest in DK; Bosch second; Samsung good via Elgiganten/whiteaway; Xiaomi weakest (no formal DK distributor chain).
